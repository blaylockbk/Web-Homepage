BYU Merit Badge Pow Wow

Each fall and spring BYU hosts a merit badge pow wow for the Boy Scouts in the area. Dozens of merit badge classes are offered. I have been the instructor for the weather merit badge class since fall 2014. More information and registration can be found on their website: http://ce.byu.edu/cw/powwow/.


Homework Website

The homework website will help you fulfill requirement 2, "Name five dangerous weather-related conditions. Give the safety rules for each when outdoors." Use this website for weblinks to learn safety rules for various types of dangerous weather.

Also on the webpage are links to the homework packet, slides, merit badge book, and homework instructions.
