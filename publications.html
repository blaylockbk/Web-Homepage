Relationship between Great Salt Lake level and lake breeze from pibal and rawinsonde observations

April 2015

In my environmental statistics class our final project was to do a statistics based research project. This paper investigates the relationship between upper level winds observated at the Salt Lake International Aiport between 1926 and 2015 and the lake level. THIS HAS NOT BEEN PUBLISHED, but I did get a good grade on it.

If you look at this time lapse of the Great Salt Lake since 1984 to present you'll see that the lake grows and shrinks from year to year. This paper explores how the lake level may influence the lake breeze winds.
